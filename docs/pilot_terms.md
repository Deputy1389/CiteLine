# Pilot Terms (Evaluation Program)

Effective date: 2026-03-04

This pilot is for technical evaluation of CiteLine only. It is not production legal advice software and not a guaranteed final product.

## Service Level
No SLA is provided during pilot. Support is best-effort.

## Support Model
Issue triage and reruns are handled as capacity allows. Response priority is based on active pilot impact.

## Data Retention
Pilot packet data and generated artifacts may be retained for up to 30 days for debugging and evaluation, then deleted on a rolling basis.

## PHI / Security
Do not treat the pilot as a HIPAA-certified environment or as a PHI guarantee program. Submit only data your organization is approved to use in a pilot context.

## Product Volatility
Pipeline behavior, quality gates, output schema, and exports may change during pilot without prior notice.

## Availability and Changes
The service may be paused, rate-limited, or modified during stabilization windows.

## Warranty / Liability
Pilot is provided as-is for evaluation. No warranty of fitness, completeness, or uninterrupted availability is provided.
